Key Insights & Findings

ASL Loss is the single most impactful change: v9-asl (0.7755 SS val) vs v5-BCE (0.7527). +0.023 SS val, +0.034 holdout AUC. Use ASL for all future experiments.
Model Soup (top-3 ckpt avg) consistently adds +0.003~0.013 holdout AUC. Always apply soup after training. v9-asl → v9-asl-soup: +0.0065.
v5 carries unique LB signal: replacing v5 with v9-asl-soup dropped LB 0.893→0.892. Keep v5 in ensemble. Best strategy: v5 + v9-asl-soup + competitor (triple SED).
Geo Filtering fully abandoned: ss_hard -0.34 AUC, ss_soft neutral, sa_weighted -0.002. No geographic post-processing helps.
PCEN hurts holdout generalization: sedp-b0-v2-fusion gets 0.7454 SS val but only 0.6737 holdout. PCEN overfits to soundscape domain, fails on train_audio holdout.
Data Leakage in holdout: All 7037 holdout files exist in train.csv. All holdout AUCs are inflated. v12-bce-soup 0.9722 is leakage artifact (SS val only 0.7263). Use SS val as true proxy.
Trick4 (zero prior) abandoned: LB dropped 0.893→0.890. T1/T2/T3 remain valid.
